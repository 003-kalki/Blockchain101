Books :)

Books are one of the best resources to learn the basics of blockchain. There is a plethora of books that cover the fundamentals, business applications, and ongoing research in blockchain. As blockchain is continuously evolving with numerous advancements aimed at improving this technology, it is essential to select the latest and most updated books for learning blockchain development. Consider that extensive research is continually being conducted in the field of blockchain, with scientists exploring various applications of this technology. Here are some of my top recommendations!



Basics of Blockchain: "Basics of Blockchain" provides a comprehensive introduction to the economics, technology, and business aspects of blockchain. It serves as an essential guide for building foundational knowledge in this transformative technology, making it accessible for readers of various backgrounds and interests.

Mastering Blockchain: The book delves into the fundamentals of cryptocurrencies, smart contracts, and decentralized applications (DApps), providing readers with a deep understanding of the underlying concepts and practical applications of blockchain. It is an essential resource for anyone looking to grasp the potential of this transformative technology. >

Decoding blockchain for business: The book helps readers grasp the core concepts of blockchain and provides insights on how organizations can leverage this technology to improve their operations and processes. It serves as a valuable resource for business professionals looking to navigate the evolving landscape of blockchain and its potential impact on various industries.

Building Blockchain Project: The book provides a step-by-step approach to building and deploying smart contracts, enabling readers to understand the practical implementation of blockchain technology. It covers the essential tools and techniques required to construct real-world blockchain projects, making it an invaluable resource for developers looking to leverage Ethereum and Solidity for decentralized application development.
